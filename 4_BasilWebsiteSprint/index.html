About

Details about Basil's life and general existence, base knowledge about him

Basil resides in a small town known as Faraway Town, a place, as the name implies, far away from most other cities and towns in the area, where he lives with his grandmother, as his parents are only stated to be too busy to be present with Basil. As his grandmother is growing old and suffering many health issues, being in a comatose state for at least one week, Basil is primarily being raised and cared for by Polly, a nurse set to take care of his grandmother whom his parents paid to care for Basil too. Basil has blonde hair, bright blue eyes, and a very pale complexion. About 4 years ago, when one of his friends met an unfortunate end, he became wracked with grief and fear, isolating himself from his friend group. As you can tell, his mental health is struggling as we interact with him in the timespan of Omori.

Personality and Habits

This section details Basil's general demeanor, his hobbies and favourite passtimes

Basil is overall a kind, timid person, who puts his friends above his own wellbeing, which has hurt him terribly. This timidness has prevent him from standing up for himself against many bullies, either due to his wish to avoid more conflict or because of his guilt and grief, he feels he deserves this punishment. He also enjoys taking care of plants, as his house is entirely filled with a diverse set of plants, proof of his proficiency in raising and caring for his greens, as many of them have been alive for over 5 years. He also enjoys photography, taking pictures of his friends, family, and as he states, the things he fears he will lose the most. He is extremely caring and trusting of his friends, willing to put himself in danger and at risk to help his friend avoid the same.

Relationships

This section details the relationships Basil has with those around him and the nature each has had.

  • Sunny: Best friends, extremely close, though a dark secret has put a rift between the two. This rift, depending on the choices of Sunny, will determine whether both live the rest of their lives, or take their own.
  • Kel: Good friends, not the closest due to their drastic differences but still care for one another and take care of each other
  • Aubrey: Complicated friendship, used to be great friends, but after something Aubrey found in Basils house, she has hated and bullied him, though they do end up coming to care for each other in the end in some endings
  • Hero: Like a father figure to Basil, though they don't interact too much, they're both caring, compassionate people, so they get along
  • Polly: Polly is Basil's caretaker, though with his grief and fear he barely interacts with her, despite her best efforts to help him open up and be happy
  • Basil's grandmother: Very close, his sole caretaker and the woman who raised Basil, though in her comatose state we cannot see this relationships strength
  • Basil's Parents: Absent from his life, unable or not willing to raise him, stated they are "too busy"
