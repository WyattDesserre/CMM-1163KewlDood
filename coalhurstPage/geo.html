Welcome to the Plains

In spite of the name, our natural world is quite beautiful, and amazing to look out upon. Come, and we will show you what we mean!

Surrounded by farms and canola fields, there is no shortage of lovely views to gaze upon, where you may look upon these views seemingly stretching endlessly over the level land and plains biome structure.

We are not too far away from Lethbridge either, so you can enjoy the shopping and all the niceties of Lethbridge whilst having the quaint living community of a small town here in Coalhurst. You can enjoy the coulees, lakes, and wonderful parks and return to Coalhurst, free of the chaos and cacophony of Lethbridge.
