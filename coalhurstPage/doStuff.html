The Important Stuff

Here in Coalhurst, we have a focus on family and raising children, and thus we have made it a focus to take your effort away from focusing on this sort of thing, taking this off your plate to give you more time with your children!

Mainly built on farming, primarily canola and cattle and the like, though Coalhurst is never too far away from Lethbridge, so you can enjoy the job opportunities of the bigger city whilst still living in the smaller town! Even if neither seems too enticing, there are always local businesses that look to hire local workers, so there are many options for employment opportunities.

There are 2 schools in Coalhurst, the Elementary school and the High school. The elementary school is a nice school, filled with tech for the students, accommodation for those who need it and an appealing design. The high school is getting an upgrade quite soon, so now is the perfect time to arrive! One consistent thing between both schools, the teachers there are top-notch, you cannot get any better!
