Our lovely residents

Ah, our residents, the people who make this town what it is. They are our lifeblood, and the people who we strive to appease the most, and we sure hope you too join the populace of our wonderful municipality!

Many families choose to call Coalhurst home, as the schools are never too far to walk to, the community is friendly, and the city is never too far for a quick drive. We know this is the case, because around 60% of our population is below the age of 18!

Not to mention, all those who work in the businesses in Coalhurst are friendly to children. For anyone looking to settle down, Coalhurst has genuinely nice and interesting characters willing to make your stay as great as possible.
