What to do

Here we will show you the vast expanses of all the things you can do in Coalhurst! Join us as we take a walk through the multitude of entertaining options you have in our fine town!

In October, the corn maze opens, with many attractions and activities to spend time on, that children adore. From the titular corn maze that changes its path every year, meaning every year you can experience it again! There are tractor rides, taking families riding on hay seats across the entire area.

To honor the town's humble roots, the town holds a parade every year. Candy is thrown, attractions are set up and ready to entertain, and once children attend their first Miners Day event, it will become another holiday for them, not dissimilar to easter and the like.

Something many people worry about to an irrational degree is getting attacked by a street animal. Well, I am incredibly happy to tell you that most of our street animals are kind and happy to see you! Our cat population is exceptionally large and adore attention from the humans going about their days, so going for a walk becomes a makeshift petting zoo of friendly kitties who want pets and attention.

At long last, the promised water park has finished construction! Have fun in your swimsuit at the memorial park water park, where you can be relieved to realize it has extra protection in the form of a fence surrounding it, so all children playing will be safe. Play in the water, splash around, and have fun in this long-awaited addition to the town.
